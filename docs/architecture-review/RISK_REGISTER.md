# AI Pipeline - Risk Register

Severity × Likelihood matrix. Reference: [AI_PIPELINE_ARCHITECTURE_REVIEW.md](AI_PIPELINE_ARCHITECTURE_REVIEW.md).

Severity scale: **S1** existential / data corruption / silent revenue loss · **S2** user-visible degradation · **S3** operational pain · **S4** cosmetic.
Likelihood: **L1** weekly · **L2** monthly · **L3** quarterly · **L4** rare.

| --- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --- | ------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |

## Top 5 to fix this quarter

1. **R1** - Filter fail-open. _Half a day. Highest blast radius._
2. **R2** - Cron idempotency / advisory lock. _Half a day. Trivial fix, huge safety win._
3. **R10** - `PipelineLlmCall` audit table. _One day. Unblocks every other observability question._
4. **R5 + R8** - Move to queue (Inngest or pg-boss). _One sprint. Removes the architectural ceiling._
5. **R4** - Prompt-injection hardening on auto-approval URLs. _One day. Cheap insurance._
